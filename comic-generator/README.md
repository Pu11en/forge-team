Transform your stories into epic comic panels!

## Features

- **Story Input**: Describe your comic narrative
- **Style Selection**: American, Manga, European, Noir, Superhero, Cartoon
- **Panel Count**: 4, 6, or 8 panels
- **Smart Breakdown**: Automatically splits story into logical panels
- **AI Prompts**: Generates ready-to-use prompts for Kie AI

## How It Works

1. Enter your story in the text area
2. Choose a comic style
3. Select number of panels
4. Click "Generate Comic"
5. Get panel breakdown with AI-ready prompts

## Integration with ForgeBot

For full AI image generation, use ForgeBot in Slack:

```
@ForgeBot generate comic panels:
Panel 1: "A brave astronaut lands on Mars", American comic style
Panel 2: "She discovers an alien city", American comic style
...
```
